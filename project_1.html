Recreating Steam

For this project I picked a site to recreate as accurate as possible and maybe even make improvements. I decided to make the online game store Steam. I had the choice to make the site responsive for different screensizes and mobile, or to give the site a more polished look along with interaction on the so-called "surface" layer. For me it was important to make my site look very close to the original, so I chose the surface layer. (The website has been coded for mobile-first and hasn't been completely made to fit normal desktop screens. So to view the site the way it's supposed to, the hotkeys CTRL + SHIFT + M can be used.)

Learning points

During this project I learned a lot more about CSS and applied the newfound knowledge to a website. It was interesting to pick a website and proceed to recreate the whole thing. The hardest components for me, were the sections where I had to put multiple items in a grid and position them. I take pride the most in the styled carousels, which can be navigated to scroll through.

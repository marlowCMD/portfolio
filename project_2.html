Rest Quest

This is a Ubiquitous Computing project or Ubicomp for short. In the chosen case I solve the problem of spending too many hours in front of a screen. The design is an IoT (Internet of Things) product that simulates interaction in the shape of a figurine that is placed on a platform, followed by an activity the user has to complete. In the final report I wrote about the interaction and functionalities of the design, backed by the Donald Norman design principles and applied persuasive patterns.

Learning points

This is the first time that I designed an Internet of Things product. I didn't know a lot about IoT and screenless products before this project. It gave me a new perspective on digital products, which is that that not every product needs a screen to support someone digitally. This project sadly didn't get realized, so it ended up staying in the concepting phase. However, I gained a lot of interest in the Internet of Things.
